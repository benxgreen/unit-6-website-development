About Envirma Education

Envirma is an American-values British school that focuses on the educational curriculum provided by the British government while keeping the values of American schools - compromising on the quality of teaching and subjects with the comfort of the students.

Envirma has a 98.54% success rate and our previous Year 11 students had all passed with a grade 4, or higher.

Envirma is located in the countryside of Britain, close to the South West in Bideford. Bideford is a historic port town on the estuary of the River Torridge in north Devon, south-west England. It is the main town of the Torridge local government district.
